The Company

Lefisome Therapeutics novel delivery technology provides a non-invasive, non-antigenic, permeable, stable, soluble and receptor-specific way to transport drugs across the BBB and into the CNS. It has potential applications in a wide number of therapeutic areas and in transport through various biological barriers.Lifesome Therapeutics technology combines protease resistance, the capacity to carry a wide range of cargoes, low production costs, and low immunogenic potential.
