    Central to our mission is the LIFESOME New Active Delivery System, and integrated and versatile nanovector for generating new possibilities to personalized treatments that respond to patient individual needs.

    From the screening of hundreds of compounds, targeting membrane proteins disease related, one of them was specific selected to form nanoparticles.

    Those nanoparticles revealed an anti-tumoral and antimetastatic activity for the first time for a carrier alone.

    Lamellar and non-lamellar lipid phases to guarantee the right formulation approach.
